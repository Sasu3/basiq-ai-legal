Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Baseek AI Auto Tec International Limited ("BASIQ AI", "we", "us", or "our") operates the BASIQ AI AutoScan 360 mobile application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Vehicle Data: OBD diagnostics including RPM, speed, coolant temperature, and battery voltage collected via Bluetooth from your vehicle's OBD-II port.
  • Photos and Videos: Images and video you capture or select for vehicle inspection analysis.
  • Payment Information: M-Pesa phone number used to process payment for PDF reports. We do not store card numbers or full payment details.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for app functionality and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze your vehicle's condition using AI and generate a PDF health report.
  • To process M-Pesa payments for report generation.
  • To improve the accuracy of our vehicle diagnostics engine.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Vehicle scan data and media are uploaded to secure servers hosted on AWS (Amazon Web Services) in the EU (Stockholm) region. Data is retained for 90 days after report generation and then permanently deleted.

4. Bluetooth Access

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to connect to the BASIQ AI-OBD device. Bluetooth is only activated when you tap "OBD Connection" and is not used for tracking or location purposes.

5. Camera and Photo Library

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to capture vehicle inspection images. These are only uploaded when you initiate a scan.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@basiqai.com. We will process all deletion requests within 14 days.

7. Children's Privacy

BASIQ AI AutoScan 360 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
