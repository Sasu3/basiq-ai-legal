Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the OBD-II port in my car?

Under the dashboard on the driver's side, near the steering column. It is a 16-pin trapezoid-shaped connector.

Does the engine need to be running?

For a full report including live engine data (RPM, temperature, voltage), the engine should be running. With engine off (key to ACC/ON), the app still collects stored fault codes, VIN, make, model, year, and mileage.

How long does a full inspection take?

A complete inspection — OBD data, photos, video, upload, analysis, and report delivery — takes under 10 minutes.

What does the report contain?

Vehicle identity (plate, VIN, make, model, year, mileage), engine diagnostics (fault codes in plain language, RPM, temperature, voltage), and visual findings (damage, paint, glass, tyres) — all stated neutrally as observed. BASIQ AI does not grade, score, or recommend.

Can I use the app without the OBD device?

Yes. You can do a visual-only inspection using photos and video. The report will contain visual findings only. Engine diagnostics require the BASIQ AI-OBD device.

I paid but did not receive my report. What do I do?

Contact us immediately on WhatsApp at +254 722 302 184 or email info@basiqai.com. We resolve payment issues within 24 hours.

My OBD device is not connecting. What do I do?

Make sure the device is fully plugged in and the indicator light is on. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone. Close and reopen the app and try scanning again. If the issue persists, WhatsApp us at +254 722 302 184.

Is my data safe?

All data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS and stored on secure AWS servers in the EU region. Data is deleted 90 days after report delivery. We do not sell your data to any third party.

What is BASIQ AI's role?

BASIQ AI is a neutral evidence company. We report only what is visible and detectable at the time of inspection. We do not grade, value, or recommend whether to buy or sell. All findings are facts for you to decide.
